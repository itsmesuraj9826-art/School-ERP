How to Login — Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to access the parent portal

1
Get Your Credentials

Contact the school office or administration. They will provide you with a Username and Password for your parent account.

2
Open the Login Page

Visit the school's ERP website using any browser on your phone or computer. Click the "Login" button on the homepage.

3
Enter Your Details

Type your Username and Password exactly as provided. Then click "Sign In". Tick "Remember me" to stay logged in.

4
Access Your Dashboard

You will be taken to your Parent Dashboard where you can see all your child's information at a glance.

About Your Login Credentials
  • Your username is usually your child's roll number or a name given by the school.
  • Your initial password is sent to your registered email or given in person by the school office.
  • If you have forgotten your password, contact the school admin via WhatsApp or phone to reset it.
  • Credentials are one parent account per student — each account is linked to one child.

Helpful Tips
  • Use Chrome or Safari for the best experience. The portal works on mobile too.
  • You can add the portal to your phone's home screen like an app — look for "Add to Home Screen" in your browser menu.
  • Check the portal regularly for fee due dates and school notices.
  • If you see any wrong information about your child, contact the school admin immediately.

Need Help?

If you face any problem logging in or have questions, contact the school administration directly via WhatsApp.
